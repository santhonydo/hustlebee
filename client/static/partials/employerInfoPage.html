How it works

HustleBee connects licensed healthcare professionals to local healthcare businesses in need of shift coverage. Get pre-vetted healthcare providers in 3 easy steps.

1. Post

Post shifts on our web portal to reach a network of qualified healthcare workers.

2. Pay

Pre-pay for your shifts to expedite payments to our workers. We accept all major credit cards.

3. Track

Manage your posted shifts 24/7 and get notifications when shifts are accepted.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pick the workers?

No. We pride ourselves on vetting the very best licensed professionals in the industry to work with us so you don't have to. If you are unsatisfied, we will be happy to offer you a full refund.

Why do you ask for full payment up-front?

We want to ensure that our workers get paid immediately after their shifts.

Can I cancel a scheduled shift?

Yes, you have full control of your posted shifts as long as they have not been accepted by our workers. Cancelling shifts after they have been accepted will be subjected to a 50% shift penalty.

How do you train your workers?

Our licensed professionals are expected to know how to perform their main duties. Employers should offer guidance at the beginning of each shift.

Is your service available in my city?

Our services are currently only available to San Francisco Bay and Metro Los Angeles Areas. You can still register for an account and we will notify you as soon as we land in your city!
